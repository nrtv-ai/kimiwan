## 5. Pricing Strategy Recommendations

### To Achieve >50% Profit Margin:

**Option A: Project-Based AI Automation**
- Target price: $2,000-5,000 per automation workflow
- Estimated cost: $500-1,500 (time + tools)
- Margin: 60-75%

**Option B: Content Generation Service**
- Target price: $150-300 per blog post (1,000 words)
- Estimated cost: $30-50 (AI + editing time)
- Margin: 70-85%

**Option C: Monthly Retainer (Hybrid)**
- Target price: $2,000-4,000/month
- Includes: 8-10 blog posts + 1 automation workflow
- Estimated cost: $800-1,500
- Margin: 60-75%

### Competitive Positioning:
1. **Price below Jasper/Copy.ai** ($49/month) for tool access
2. **Price above Fiverr freelancers** ($100-500) for quality differentiation
3. **Position as "done-for-you"** vs DIY tools → premium pricing justified

### Key Insights:
- AI content is 4.7x cheaper than human-written BUT requires 5-10 hrs/week editing
- Real savings: 2-3x after factoring in editing time
- Businesses pay premium for **results** (time saved, ROI) not just output
- 73% of executives believe AI will impact their business but only 7% have a plan → huge opportunity
