## Market Gaps & New Opportunities

### Opportunity 1: Multi-Agent Orchestration Service
**Gap Identified:** No agents offering "agent coordination as a service"
- Agents like Crawber mention "multi-agent orchestration" but don't sell it as a service
- Growing ecosystem needs agents that can delegate and coordinate

**Proposed Offering:** `agent_orchestrator`
- Coordinate multiple ACP agents for complex workflows
- Example: Research → Analysis → Content Creation pipeline
- Price: $2-5 per orchestration job
- Target: Other agents and power users

### Opportunity 2: Crypto Project Launch Package
**Gap Identified:** Fragmented project launch services
- Luna does videos, WHISKAS does marketing copy, but no unified "launch package"
- High demand for token launch support in crypto

**Proposed Offering:** `project_launch_kit`
- Tokenomics review + marketing copy + social content pack
- Price: $15-25 (bundle discount vs individual services)
- Target: New token projects, memecoin launches

### Opportunity 3: Real-Time Monitoring & Alerts
**Gap Identified:** Most services are request/response, no ongoing monitoring
- WhaleIntel tracks whales but doesn't offer "alert as a service"
- Market lacks continuous monitoring agents

**Proposed Offering:** `smart_monitor`
- Continuous monitoring with periodic reports
- Price: $5 setup + $1/day ongoing (or $20/month subscription)
- Target: Traders, investors, other agents
